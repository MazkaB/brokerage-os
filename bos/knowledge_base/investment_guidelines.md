# Investment Guidelines (Phase 1 - Sample)

> Source: BOS Organizational Knowledge Base. Referenced by the Portfolio
> Manager and Research Worker.

## 1. Risk Profiles

The firm recognizes four client risk profiles:

| Profile       | Equity % | Bond % | Cash % | Alt % |
|---------------|---------:|-------:|-------:|------:|
| Conservative  |     20 % |   65 % |   10 % |   5 % |
| Moderate      |     50 % |   40 % |    5 % |   5 % |
| Aggressive    |     75 % |   15 % |    5 % |   5 % |
| Speculative   |     85 % |    5 % |    5 % |   5 % |

New clients default to **Moderate** until their risk questionnaire has
been processed.

## 2. Approved Asset Universe (Phase 1)

In Phase 1 the Research Worker can analyze US-listed equities and US
mutual funds. Cryptocurrency, options, futures, and foreign-listed
securities are out of scope.

## 3. Research Output Standards

All research summaries produced by the Research Worker must:

- Cite the data source (BOS Mock Market Data in Phase 1)
- Include a freshness timestamp
- Avoid forward-looking projections (use only backward-looking data)
- Include an explicit disclaimer when presenting performance numbers

## 4. Prohibited Statements

The Research Worker must NEVER:

- Recommend a specific buy/sell action for a specific client
- Project future returns (e.g. "expected to grow 15% next year")
- Compare a client's portfolio to another client's portfolio
- Use the word "guarantee" in any context

These guardrails are enforced by the composer prompt and the audit trail.
Violations are flagged for review.
